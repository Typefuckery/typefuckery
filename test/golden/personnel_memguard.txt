Memguard
Division: Rust
Id: rust:memguard
Starting CC: 4

Abilities:
  - Passive - Once per round, Prevent up to 1 CC loss on this Personnel
  - Passive - Once per round, when a card effect would reduce CC for this Personnel (not from spending CC): you may prevent up to 2 CC loss on this Personnel
  - -CC (3) - Spend 3 CC, then Prevent up to 1 CC loss on a Personnel
  - Burnout - When this Personnel's CC is reduced to 0: Prevent up to 1 CC loss on all Personnel in this Personnel's sector
