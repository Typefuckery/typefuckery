Shift Supervisor
Division: Institute
Id: institute:shift_supervisor
Starting CC: 4

Abilities:
  - Passive - Once per round, Prevent up to 1 CC loss on all Personnel in this Personnel's sector
  - -CC (2) - Spend 2 CC, then Flip a chosen sector to Secure
